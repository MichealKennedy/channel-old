A couple searching for an alternative to the 9-to-5 life.

We're depressed and unhappy with our 9-to-5 lives. So we're learning how to sail and looking for a sailboat to purchase. We’re going to try to live 100% off the grid, land, and sea; eliminate all our bills, and try to survive on as little money per month as possible.

Each episode will follow us (Mike & Michelle) as we sail the Canadian/US west coast and slowly all over the world. Through trial and error, we’ll learn how to fish, forage, filter water; make tools, weapons, shelters, soaps; and deal with storms, wild animals, hunger, dangerous places, low funds, and more.

Our Mission

Our long-term goals and why we're doing this.

Own our home and vehicle

To own our home and vehicle, debt-free, with no monthly mortgages or car payments, and be able to relocate to anywhere in the world we want.

100% Off The Grid

To be completely self-suffience with solar panels, rainwater catchment, solar stills, hand-cranked tools, etc.

Eliminate All Bills

To have no major bills: no rent, no power bill, no water bill, no phone bills, no grocery bill.

Fish, Forage, Hunt, Grow

To supplement (and perhaps one day live entirely) on the food that we acquire ourselves - like seafood, sprouts, berries, plants, etc.

Make Our Own Money

To pay for bills we can't get rid of, and for our own use, we're going to explore building homemade crafts like furniture, soaps, primitive tools, as well as other ideas.

Travel Forever

Once fully self-suffient and with some savings, to be able to travel the world virtually forever, with only short yearly trips back to BC, Canada to save up again.
